About MetheneTrace

This project is used to monitor and trace the presence of methane in a specified geographic area.

Major Objectives of the project are, to assess the concentration of methane. To identify areas with high concentration of methane for adminstratiev advice and action.

This is work in Progress, if you love our work be kind to support in someway...

How Should I go About this App

Have an experience with our product by following the below steps...

Click to select data -> Country -> Town

You will see the map change and results appearing at the Right side of the app

What do these Outputs mean?..

Upon Navigating the App you will see Map change and a Time series chart plot.

The Map Layer shows the region selected with varying concentrations of methane. The chart is a brief analytical indicative tool for quick high level understanding of the methane concentration.
